Harry’s Tools Story

The model builder is the first offering from Harry Tools. Harry Tools was created by a technologist turned private investor to help better manage our family office. As we work to build our portfolio of real estate and other investments, it became clear to me that there were few reasonable priced tools for high net worth individuals and family offices that offered professional level management of investment unless you were willing pay high fees for complex software systems or spend more time than you want with spreadsheets. When you’re working on investments that represent a meaning portion your net-worth, accuracy and details matters. Furthermore, the less time you can spend in “the weeds” collecting and organizing information, the more time you have to view the bigger picture. That is the mission of Harry’s Tools, to build analysis tools that are offered at a fair price, does not require a steep learning curve to use and that delivers professional grade solutions consistent our customers’ needs for comprehensive and accurate analysis.
