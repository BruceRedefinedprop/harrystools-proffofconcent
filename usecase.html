Use Cases

Solutions for High Net Worth Individuals and Family Offices

High Net Worth individuals and family offices often purchase real estate for a variety of reasons. Harry Tools will enable our customers, regardless of their skill level with Microsoft Excel to focus on analyzing their potential investment in a property rather than having to concentrate on the details of programing an Excel model or having to modify an existing model, over and over again. After creating a model, if you don’t like the format, either select and generate new model or modify your Excel model directly. If your rent, price or other key you can update the model by modifying the property’s entry form.

Our models include formats for single tenant properties, syndication deals, multi-family, mixed use properties, industrial and retail formats. If your spreadsheet breaks, regardless of the cause - user error, broken links or edits gone wrong, just generate a new model and your back in business.

Solutions for Developers and Renovators

We also a provide tool to create short-term models that enable developers to analyze their profit potential and potential risks. In these models, developer can quickly enter purchase information, hard and soft cost estimates and terminal value as well as perform sensitivity analysis based on various timing, selling price and financing cost options. Developers can also quickly test value propositions for build and sell vs. build and hold strategies.

Solutions for Syndicators and General Partnerships

The syndication models and partnerships are popular ways to invest. However, while important how the property performs, it equally important how much cash ends up in your account. In all of models, we offer the options to additionally model specific cash flows for both a general partner or limit partner assuming preferred equity and promote splits approach.
